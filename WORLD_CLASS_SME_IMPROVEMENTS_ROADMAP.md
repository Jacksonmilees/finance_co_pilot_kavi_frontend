## üìä What SMEs Need (Research Findings)

### Top 10 SME Pain Points:
1. **Limited Access to Capital** - Hard to get loans/financing
2. **Cash Flow Management** - Don't know if they'll have money next month
3. **Manual Data Entry** - Time-consuming, error-prone
4. **Tax Compliance** - Complex, confusing, expensive
5. **Invoice Management** - Late payments, tracking issues
6. **Expense Tracking** - Losing receipts, forgetting expenses
7. **Financial Reporting** - Don't understand their numbers
8. **Bank Reconciliation** - Time-consuming, error-prone
9. **Multi-Currency** - Dealing with different currencies
10. **Mobile Access** - Need to manage on-the-go
